Favorite Climbing Spots

I love climbing. Me and my friends go out almost every weekend and find new routes to climb. Its super fun and a good workout too.

St. George

St. George is probably my favorite place to climb. The rock is super good and there are tons of routes. We usually go in the spring or fall because the summer is way too hot.

  1. Devils Crack
    • 5.10c
    • Trad climb
    • One of the best routes there
  2. Wonder Boy
  3. Piss Ant

This is me on Devils Crack. It was a really hard lead but I made it to the top. Working hard or hardly working lol.

Rock Canyon

Rock Canyon is really close to where I live so we go there a lot. Its a great spot for beginners and also has some harder routes if you want a challenge.

  1. Curious George
  2. Left Serpent
  3. PA Daddy

These are my top 3 routes at Rock Canyon. Curious George is the best one in my opinion, highly recommend if you ever go there.
